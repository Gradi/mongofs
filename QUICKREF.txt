MongoFs is a Dokany app that mounts mongodb's content as filesystem.

/ -- lists databases

/database -- lists collections in 'database' database.

/database/collection -- shows meta information about 'collection' collection
    stats.json
    indexes.json

    data/

    query/<query>
    query/<query>/[0-9]+\.[bson|json]
    query/<query>/all.json

    query/<field name>/<query>
    query/<field name>/<query>/[0-9]+\.[bson|json]
    query/<field name>/<query>/all.json
